SONG: EMMITT JAMES "FUCKS WITCHU" FEAT. FIVY

We fucks with LA golden boy, Emmitt James. And we hope you do too. James, originally from Milwaukee, took his dreams to the coast and has been making waves. He somehow knows how to speak to what #mood you may find yourself in. The man has so much talent, and so much fire that he's practically releasing a new song every week. We'll take em! His latest tracks hit the nail on the head.
