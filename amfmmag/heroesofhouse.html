Metro and Smart Bar Present: Heroes of House

House music and Chicago have a long history together, from the deep roots of heavy bass, to the legendary likes of the late Frankie Knuckles. In the spirit of house music and the changing seasons, Metro and Smart Bar are throwing one of the biggest house music events this city has seen, “Heroes of House” featuring some of Chicago’s biggest and most legendary house musicians.

The event will take over the entire building this Friday April 17. Featured acts include Mark Farina and his jazz meets hip hop spins, global DJ Sneak with his latin infused beats, Derrick Carter, a Chicago favorite who claimed his fame on the turntables in the early 90’s, acid house DJ, Phuture, DJ Pierre, underground legend Michael Serafini, and Smart Bar resident DJ of Queen!, Garrett David. There will be multiple rooms with music flowing from every speaker. Be prepared to party until the wee hours of the morning, for Mark Farina has been known to do sets that have lasted eight hours.

This will surely be an event not to miss if you are a house head, music fan in general, or a person with a body that likes to move to the rhythm of the beat. A portion of the proceeds will be donated to the Frankie Knuckles Foundation. Get your tickets here, and for more information check out metrochicago.com or smartbarchicago.com.
