Artists are asked to pick a facet of themselves that they feel they want to explore, dispel, or that often gets misrepresented. Or they may choose to explore multiple facets of their identity representing how they intersect. If someone were to ask if they could see your ID what would you want to show? What do you want people to see, or how do you think people will perceive you based upon what you “tell” or “show” them in showing them your ID?  Can I See Your ID in a way is someone asking you who you are, how you either show
yourself to the world, who you wish to be,
or who the world sees you as.
