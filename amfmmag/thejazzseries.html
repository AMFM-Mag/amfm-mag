THE JAZZ SERIES



The Jazz Series is a pop up music and art showcase that takes over galleries and spaces. "We pop up. You pop out."

The series features up and coming musicians who play music with a jazz and urban and modern flair, think "trap jazz."

"this ain't your grandfather's jazz!"

 
For each show, a visual artist will take over the space with a solo exhibition, and musicians will grace your ears.
