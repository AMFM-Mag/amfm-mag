Urban dictionary defines a boi as a word derived from queer culture meaning a lesbian, or an androgonous female. But we know that boi can mean so much more. AMFM and Boi Society teamed up to create "We Dem Bois" to celebrate queer women who are comfortable in their own skin and self. To celebrate bois, tomboys and individual style. It is a rendition of the Wiz Kalifah song, "We Dem Boys," which has always been a favorite, and later the Los Rakas version of the song celebrating Latin bike culture. This one is for the queers. This special feature includes the music lifestyle video, the photo shoot, some Q&As we have done in the past highlighting queer artists and some dapper duds.
