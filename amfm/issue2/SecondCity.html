SECOND CITY

Photos and thoughts by Adrian Quezaire

Adrian Quezaire, a Milwaukee-based film/video maker, editor, decided to take his relationship with the lens to the next level. Currently working on film two projects, a short film titled 'Sour Note' about a struggling street musician, and a documentary titled 'Sogal' concerning a Somali store owner and his family living in post 9/11 america, photography was initially a means for funding. Now, for Quezaire, photography has become a full-blown hobby, and helps keep his eyes sharp and fresh. Now you can find him behind a camera of all sorts.

"I realized that filmmakers and photographers have a lot in common; however, one major difference [is] filmmakers deal in sustained light to produce an image, whereas photographers deal in bursts of light to produce images. Photographers have to convey a lot of information in a half second photo."
