ARTIST OF THE MONTH: KIM LOPER

Kim Loper self describes herself on her blog as a “Twenty something year old graduate with a heart existing somewhere between the Midwest, Brazil, and places she’s never been.” She is consumed by “art, language, beats, ladies, books, cynicism, fashion and food.” All of this is reflected on her canvas. FREAKO RICO: FREAK LIKE ME

With a nickname like Freako Rico, you definitely have to be a character. The man behind the nickname is Atlanta artist, Richard Montgomery. He got the name from classmates who knew him for his relationships with the ladies...He admits that he didn’t like the name at first, but later began to fully embrace it and almost brand himself.
