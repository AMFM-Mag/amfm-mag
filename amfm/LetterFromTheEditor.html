It's been a busy month creating the first issue of amfm: arts. music. fashion. magazine. I hope you have become as artistically, musically, and fashionably inclined as I have creating the articles for this month. From spending a weekend in Chicago being part of He Say She Say's entourage, to discovering new vintage finds and rising designers and artists, it has been a ride. I would like to give a special thanks to everyone involved in the making of this magazine and all of the great people I spoke with to create the articles you are reading in this issue. A big thank you to Mark Riechers for his amazing technical support.

As you peruse the website check out 13 great articles on some of our favorite fashion causes, music, and artists. As the year comes to an end, amfm looked back to some of the best albums of 2009. See if some of your favorites made the top 20 and listen to the song picks from the albums on our listening page. He Say She Say and Sleeping in the Aviary should definitely be added to your 2010 music library! Check out our features on them in music.

I talked to three up and coming designers and found out everything from what inspires them to what makes them tick. These are going to be the next fashion divas behind the runway minus the diva attitude. There are some people taking fashion to the next level. In our "Clothes with a Cause" article we feature four different apparel companies that are creating clothes for the greater good. If you think thats good, take a look at some of our everyday fashion models in our "Scene N' Style" lookbook.

Make sure you "discover" Nancy Hoffman in our "Undiscovered" article in the arts section. We've got all kinds of art for you this month. We've got Warhol; we've got photography; we've got tattoos and even blood! Yes, blood...Even if it creeps you out a little bit, check out the article on Trevin Prince and his unconvential art medium. Talent runs in his veins.
