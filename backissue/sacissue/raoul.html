PHOTO FEATURE: RAOUL ORTEGA

WITH WORDS AND IMAGES FROM RAOUL ORTEGA

Sacramento’s unofficial photographer would have to be Raoul Ortega. The Sac Issue wouldn’t be complete without at least one photo from his collection, and here we have five!

Raoul has photographed much of the Sacramento music scene, former popular band The New Humans, Dusty Brown, and of course Sister Crayon. In fact he did the photos for the November spread in Nylon featuring the local band.

It’s easy to see why his favorite photos he chose were all of musicians and shows around the city, an area where he truly stands out. I mean just check out his Daedelus photo from the Sacramento Electronic Music Festival. It’s majestic.

This is just the beginning for Ortega. He’s bound to do big things and go many places with his camera in hand. He took the time out to share some of his favorite shots and his words with amfm and the motivation behind the film.
