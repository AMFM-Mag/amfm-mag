ANDY WARHOL: THE LAST DECADE

22 years after his death, comes an exhibit that explores the final years of the man that introduced Pop Art to the world, Andy Warhol. The Milwaukee Art Museum is the first U.S. museum to showcase the late work of Warhol made just before his death. When Warhol died in 1987, the world lost a great art and pop culture icon. Despite his waning success in the late seventies, Warhol made some of his most profound pieces in the eighties, especially in the last two years that he was living...READ MORE

Tattoos used to be viewed as a desecration to our bodies, and only associated with old war veterans and sailors. In the meantime they have literally become a canvases for works of art. Tattoos have definitely become more artistic than your typical skull and bones, or banner with an ex's name.
